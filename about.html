About

Why I started Penny Picks

I started Penny Picks because I noticed that a lot of budget tech articles either recommend the most expensive option or repeat the same specifications without explaining what they actually mean. I wanted to make something simpler that even a beginner could understand.

What makes this site different?

  • I focus on the features that actually make a difference in day-to-day use.
  • If a cheaper product is good enough, I'll say that instead of pushing expensive options.
  • I update guides whenever I find better information or new products worth mentioning.

How I work on these guides

This is currently a personal project that I manage in my free time. To keep the website running, you may see ads or affiliate links in the future. If that happens, it won't change my opinions or recommendations.
