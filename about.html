About

Why Penny Picks exists

Penny Picks is a small, independent site with one job: help people buy budget tech without getting burned by inflated spec sheets or recycled "top 10" lists. Every guide is written to answer one question — what actually predicts whether this budget product will hold up — rather than just repeating manufacturer marketing copy.

What we do differently

  • We tell you which specs are genuine predictors of quality and which are marketing filler.
  • We're upfront when the honest answer is "just buy the cheapest option" rather than pushing you toward a specific purchase.
  • We update guides as the market changes rather than leaving stale recommendations up indefinitely.

How the site is run

Penny Picks is currently a one-person project, built and maintained independently. The site may display advertising to help cover hosting and time costs. Advertising does not influence which products or brands are discussed in our guides.
